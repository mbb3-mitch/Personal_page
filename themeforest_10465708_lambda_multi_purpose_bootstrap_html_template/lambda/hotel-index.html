The Spa Center

The Lambda Spa Center with years of experience in the field, is now the epitome of relaxation, detoxication, invigoration, purification, rejuvenation and general well-being.

The centre has an indoor swimming pool, two aquastream pools with warm seawater, a whirlpool, solarium, sanarium, sauna and Hamam as well as a total of 24 treatment rooms.
