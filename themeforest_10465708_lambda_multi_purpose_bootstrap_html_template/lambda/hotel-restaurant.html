5 star menu

Three outstanding restaurants and 2 bars await to tempt our guests with international culinary delights. Sumptuous buffets are available in the «Lambda» Restaurant for breakfast and dinner.

Cocktail Bar

Our «Lambda Bar» is offering magnificent views to the sea and our uniquely designed «Pool Bar», located in the heart of the resort next to the main swimming pool with beautiful views of the sea, offers refreshing cocktails throughout the day.

Choose from a wide selection of light snacks or treat yourself to an assortment of home made pastries to complement a morning coffee, while reading your newspaper.
